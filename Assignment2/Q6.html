Delicious Biryani

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Basmati rice
  • 500g Chicken or Mutton
  • 1 large Onion, sliced
  • 2 Tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons Ginger-garlic paste
  • Spices: Bay leaves, Cinnamon, Cloves, Cardamom
  • 1/4 cup Yogurt
  • 1/2 cup Fresh coriander leaves, chopped
  • 1/4 cup Ghee or oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (for cooking rice)

Steps

  1. Wash and soak the Basmati rice for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a large pot and fry the sliced onions until golden brown.
  3. Add the ginger-garlic paste, chicken/mutton, and cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Mix in chopped tomatoes, spices, yogurt, and cook for another 10 minutes.
  5. In a separate pot, cook the rice with water and a pinch of salt until 70% done.
  6. Layer the partially cooked rice over the meat mixture and cook on low heat for 15-20 minutes.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot!

Nutritional Information

  • Calories per serving: 450-500 kcal
  • Rich in proteins (from chicken/mutton)
  • Provides a good amount of carbohydrates (from rice)
  • Contains healthy fats (from ghee or oil)

Fun Fact

Biryani originated from the Indian subcontinent and is believed to have been brought by the Mughals from Persia!
